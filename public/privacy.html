Receipt 2 meal Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Receipt 2 meal ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our web application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Pantry Data: Ingredient names, quantities, expiry dates, and storage locations you manually enter or import via receipt scanning.
  • Profile Preferences: Household size, dietary goals, allergens, cuisine preferences, and cooking skill level.
  • Meal Plans: AI-generated recipes and your feedback on them.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Anonymous Authentication ID: We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to associate your data with a unique session ID. No email, name, or password is required.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics such as features used and session duration (anonymized).
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device type, and IP address (for security purposes only).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Receipt 2 meal service
  • To generate personalized AI meal plans based on your pantry inventory
  • To notify you about expiring ingredients
  • To improve our AI recipe recommendations
  • To analyze and reduce food waste patterns (aggregated and anonymized)

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore, encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • Access to your data is restricted by Firebase Security Rules — only your authenticated session can read or modify your data.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase: Authentication and database hosting (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Google Gemini AI: Used for recipe generation and ingredient parsing (subject to Google AI Privacy)

5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app at any time.
  • Deletion: You can permanently delete all your data using the "Clear All Data" button in the Analytics tab.
  • Portability: You can export your pantry data at any time.
  • Opt-out: Since we use anonymous authentication, simply clearing your browser data disassociates you from your stored data.

6. Cookies

Receipt 2 meal uses local storage (not cookies) to maintain your session and preferences. No tracking cookies are used.

7. Children's Privacy

Receipt 2 meal is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@fridgechef.app
